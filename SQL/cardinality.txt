//Database relationship 
There are three types of database relationships:
1. One-to-one : e.g. - one country can have on representative
2. One-to-many : e.g. - One user can have multiple posts.  It is further classified into three categories:
    a. One to few : e.g. - one account has multiple address in zomato (0-100)
    b. One to much :(1000-10 lakh)
    c. One to many : more than 10 lakh
3. Many-to-many: e.g. - One student can have multiple subjects, and one subject can be assigned to multiple students.
